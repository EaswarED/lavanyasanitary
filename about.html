WHO WE ARE IN THE MODERN WORLD

Lavanya Sanitary Centre was established in the year 1984. It’s located in the Capital of Kanyakumari district - Nagercoil. We are trading Tiles, Sanitary, Kitchen and Paints, the 4 decades of unmatched contributions to its credit. The construction industry has been tremendous growth over these years. In spite of the highly competitive market that exists today, Lavanya Sanitary Centre continues to glitter as the choicest name among the trusted service providers in the modern world. Let it be the flooring tiles, wall tiles, bath room ceramics or the bathroom accessories, the ranges are vast and also having leading brand sanitary fittings and accessories with chromium plated. Lavanya Sanitary Centre offer Modular Kitchen leading brand accessories. It starts from the normal affordable brands to the most sensational globally renowned brands. We have it a state of the art multi global brand. We have the palatial Luxury mansion or a common man’s residential consolidation, Lavanya Sanitary Centre has a range each to choose from. Lavanya is known for its respect for tradition, the values it maintains and its business ethics. This could, probably be the reason behind the mass trust bestowed upon Lavanya. Today Lavanya has become a household name for the construction industry and also the living community in Tamilnadu. Quality, Reliability and the Trustworthiness are the three values that make the symbol of Lavanya fly high.
